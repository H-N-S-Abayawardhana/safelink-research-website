Assessment Timeline

Key milestones and deliverables for our research project across both semesters.

September 2025

Project Proposal

Initial proposal presentation covering project scope, objectives, literature review, and proposed methodology. This milestone establishes the foundation and direction of the research project.

Marks: 6%
January 2026

Progress Presentation 1

First progress review demonstrating initial implementation, data collection progress, and preliminary model training results. Includes a 50% completion status report.

Marks: 15%
March 2026

Progress Presentation 2

Second progress review showcasing advanced implementation, integrated system modules, testing results, and 80% project completion with evaluation metrics.

Marks: 18%
April 2026

Final Presentation and Viva

Final project presentation and viva evaluation covering the complete system demonstration, research contributions, implementation details, and individual understanding of all project components.

Marks: 10%
